# 🏋️ Gamified Fitness Tracker

A fun, gamified workout application that turns your fitness journey into an engaging game. Track workouts, earn points, level up, and maintain streaks - all in your browser!

## ✨ Features

- 🎮 **Gamified Workouts** - Earn XP and level up as you complete exercises
- 📊 **Track Progress** - Monitor your daily, weekly, and monthly workout history
- 🔥 **Streak System** - Build motivation with consecutive day tracking
- 🏆 **Achievement Badges** - Unlock rewards for reaching fitness milestones
- 💾 **Local Storage** - Your progress saves automatically in your browser
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
